### Big picture: what this buys you

This dataset isn’t just “108 random examples” – it’s effectively a **mini language for GRC policy work**:

* six transformation *modes* (`engineer_simplification`, `obligation_extraction`, `policy_to_controls`, `term_harmonization`, `merge_deduplicate`, `exec_summary`),
* applied across the main GRC domains you care about (access, IAM, vendors, privacy, IR, BCDR, cloud, vuln, etc.),
* using realistic style snippets (policies, standards, procedures, charters, contract excerpts).

Fine-tuning Mistral on this gives you a model that can:

* read almost any GRC/policy text you throw at it, and
* respond in **one of these structured, highly useful formats**.

That’s exactly the kind of “GRC copilot brain” you want sitting behind your infosec agent stack.
